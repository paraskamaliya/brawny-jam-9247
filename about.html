About US

Welcome to TechSchool.com, your premier destination for specialized technical education and training. At TechSchool, we are committed to providing students with the skills, knowledge, and hands-on experience necessary to excel in today's ever-evolving technological landscape.

Our tech school is dedicated to offering a diverse range of industry-focused programs that cater to various fields of study. Whether you're passionate about software development, engineering, healthcare, design, or any other technical discipline, we have a program tailored to suit your interests and career goals.

Our curriculum is carefully crafted in collaboration with industry experts to ensure that you receive the most up-to-date and relevant education. We prioritize hands-on training, providing you with ample opportunities to apply your knowledge in real-world scenarios. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated workshops allow you to gain practical experience using the latest tools, technologies, and equipment.

At TechSchool, we understand the importance of qualified instructors. Our faculty consists of experienced professionals who have extensive industry backgrounds and a passion for teaching. They bring their practical expertise into the classroom, providing you with invaluable insights and mentorship throughout your educational journey.

One of the key advantages of attending TechSchool is the accelerated nature of our programs. We recognize that many individuals are eager to enter the workforce quickly, so our courses are designed to provide you with the necessary skills in a shorter timeframe. This means you can graduate and embark on your professional career sooner than if you pursued a traditional four-year degree.

Our commitment to your success extends beyond the classroom. We offer comprehensive career services to assist you in finding employment opportunities after graduation. From resume writing workshops to job placement assistance, we're dedicated to helping you launch your career in your chosen field.

TechSchool.com embraces a flexible approach to education, recognizing that our students have diverse commitments and schedules. We offer both full-time and part-time options, as well as evening classes, allowing you to balance your education with other personal or professional obligations.

Upon completion of your program at TechSchool, you'll not only possess a wealth of technical knowledge but also industry-recognized certifications and credentials. These credentials will set you apart in the job market, demonstrating your expertise and dedication to your chosen field.

Join us at TechSchool.com and embark on an exciting educational journey that will equip you with the skills needed to thrive in today's tech-driven world. Discover endless opportunities, unleash your potential, and pave the way to a successful and rewarding career.

Courses

Computer Programming

Duration: 12 months

Learn programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++, and develop the skills to design and build software applications. Topics covered include algorithms, data structures, web development, and software testing.

Electrical Engineering

Duration: 18 months

Gain a strong foundation in electrical engineering principles, circuit design, and electronic systems. Learn to analyze and troubleshoot electrical systems, work with power distribution, and design control systems.

Plumbing and HVAC

Duration: 9 months

Master the skills required for plumbing and heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems. Learn about pipe fitting, installation, repair, and maintenance of plumbing and HVAC systems, including safety regulations and energy efficiency.

Graphic Design

Duration: 12 months

Explore the world of visual communication and design. Develop skills in Adobe Creative Suite, typography, layout design, and branding. Learn to create compelling graphics for various media platforms, including print and digital.

Automotive Technology

Duration: 15 months

Acquire hands-on experience in automotive repair and maintenance. Learn about engine diagnostics, electrical systems, brake systems, and automotive technology advancements. Gain knowledge of hybrid and electric vehicles.

Medical Assisting

Duration: 9 months

Prepare for a career in healthcare by learning essential skills for medical assisting. Study medical terminology, patient care techniques, medical office administration, and clinical procedures. Gain practical experience through internships.
